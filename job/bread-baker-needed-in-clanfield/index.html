Bread Baker needed in Clanfield

Blake’s Kitchen

We are looking for an artisan baker at our original bakery in Clanfield.  You will be producing real bread, including award-winning sourdough and pastries. 

You will be baking consistently excellent products to sell in our café shop, and for our chefs to produce outstanding sandwiches and breakfasts with. We have some exciting plans for growth in the coming months, which will provide great opportunities for development for the successful candidate.

Absolutely everything in our kitchen is made by us, by hand, freshly every day, on site.

You will be happy with very early starts; as at the moment we do not work night shifts.  A typical shift time may be 4 am until 1pm and will include some weekends.

Whilst professional bread baking experience is not necessary, we will want to see a real passion for baking, attention to detail and an ability to work well under pressure.

In return we can offer excellent pay and support with ongoing professional development, access to an external Employee Advice Programme, free lunch on shift, staff discount scheme, and the possibilities of progression within a growing business.
